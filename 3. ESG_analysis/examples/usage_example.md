# Usage Example

**User Input:**
"Compare the 2024 ESG performance of TSMC and Intel."

**Agent Action:**
1. **Search**: Finds TSMC 2024 Sustainability Report and Intel 2023/24 CSR Report.
2. **Extract**: Reads the files/pages to find GHG emissions, water usage, and diversity stats.
3. **Draft**: Fills out the `comparison_template.md`.

**Sample Output Snippet:**
